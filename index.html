Salute to our frontline warriors

What they've done for us!

Doctors and other health-care workers are working excruciatingly during these hard times. They are on the frontlines working with a high risk wearing the painful PPE kits, staying away from friends and family. It's difficult to think about the consequenses without their hardwork and sacrifices.
The entire police force are responsible for maintaining order during the lockdown in order to ensure people follow social-distancing. They are also at high risk of coming in contact with COVID affected people.
Government of India took all necessary steps to face the challenge and threat posed by COVID-19. They empowered the citizens with the right information and took precautions as per advisories issued by Ministry of Health & Family Welfare.
